## 🚀 Vision

Our vision is to create an indispensable AI-powered co-pilot for researchers. MindGraph AI will transform the arduous process of literature review into an accelerated journey of discovery, enabling users to quickly understand complex fields, identify seminal works, and uncover novel research opportunities.

---

## 🏛️ Core Features (The Four Pillars)

The project is structured around four integrated pillars of analysis:

1.  **Knowledge Foundation:** Automated construction of a rich knowledge graph from research papers, capturing concepts, authors, methods, and their interconnections.
2.  **Influence Mapping:** Advanced network analysis to identify true intellectual influence, moving beyond simple citation counts to find the most important papers, authors, and ideas.
3.  **Temporal & Thematic Analysis:** Visualization of a research field's evolution over time, highlighting emerging trends and paradigm shifts.
4.  **Opportunity Discovery & Synthesis:** Actively identifying research gaps, unexplored connections, and conflicting findings to point researchers toward novel contributions, and assisting in the generation of summaries and literature reviews.

---

## 🗺️ Development Phases

The project will be executed in four distinct phases:

-   **Phase 1: MVP - Influence & Centrality Engine**
    -   **Focus:** Build and launch the core functionality for graph creation and influence analysis. The goal is to deliver immediate value and validate the core hypothesis with a usable product.
    -   **Status:** In Progress.

-   **Phase 2: Temporal & Thematic Intelligence**
    -   **Focus:** Introduce time-based analysis and thematic clustering to show how research fields evolve.

-   **Phase 3: Opportunity Discovery & AI Synthesis**
    -   **Focus:** Implement the "monster" features: gap analysis, contradiction detection, and AI-assisted writing tools.

-   **Phase 4: Collaboration, Scale & Enterprise**
    -   **Focus:** Introduce user accounts, collaboration features, persistent storage, and prepare the platform for larger-scale adoption.
